About

Our Story

The healthybird service began with a challenge among two friends, nutritionist Amy Smith and fitness coach Adam Stone: Can we eat the way want while maintaining a healthy body?

Consulting research and experts in both the culinary arts and nutrition, our founders created healthybird to share this lifestyle with health-minded foodies like you! Delicious and nutritious food is our jam and our team is ready to help you eat well and feel well.

Our Values

Our core values at healthybird are focused on the health of our clients and the quality of our food. Let's take care of ourselves and our planet!

Delicious

We pride ourselves in providing you tasty recipes

Healthy

Sustainable
