Our People

Get to know some of the amazing members of the healthybird team who are excited to help you start your healthy-eating journey with us!

Grace Abebe

Arturo is a fitness coach and a retired soccer player. He loves to volunteer and is passionate about food accessibility.

Gerald Thomson

Gerald is a nutritionist and an avid swimmer. He loves to develop new recipes for desserts and is a co-host of the podcast "Just Desserts".

Jenna Ma

Jenna is a seasoned chef and a hiking-enthusiast. She loves to use fresh locally-sourced veggies in her cooking, and sometimes dabbles in foraging.
